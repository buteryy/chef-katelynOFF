Almond Tofu Pudding for Sunday Dinners For The Hungry! Today I made an Asian almond pudding for the hungry. For the last Sunday of the month I made desserts that are soothing and refreshing. I placed a small fruit in the middle of the pudding. Almond tofu pudding is the perfect dessert after a warm comforting meal! Using some boxed pudding, I used it and heated it up with water. Then, I placed it in the small molds and waited for them to cool and then placed it in the fridge. Finally, you can take them out of the containers and place them in a serving dish. I definitely recommend almond tofu pudding as a dessert after dinner!
