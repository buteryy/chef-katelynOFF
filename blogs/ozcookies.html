March 23, 2024

As a Bakers Club member, I made Oz-themed royal icing cookies and red velvet cheesecake brownies for the closing night of our Wizard of Oz musical, inspired by the iconic 1939 film, topping last year’s Mean Girls! I also made a few personalized “Sarah” cookies for my friend, the amazing Dorothy. Though some icing didn’t fully dry, parents loved them, with one mom saying, “OMG, they’re so cute!” My brownies sold out by intermission, and with other baked goods, we raised significant funds for our sister school in Kenya. It was a spectacular night with an outstanding cast and crew!
