May 9, 2025 - Happy Teacher Appreciation Week!

Surprise for my mom’s ladies night with my baked goods! To show my appreciation for my teachers, I decided to bake personalized pencil cookies with each teacher’s name on them. This project was so much fun! I found the pencil cookie idea online and couldn’t wait to try it. The teachers were thrilled when I handed them out—one even said her cookie would be featured in her Instagram post tonight! Two others mentioned they’d bring their cookies home to show their wives, which made me laugh.

A special thank you to my mom for helping me package each pencil cookie in a bag, making it so easy to hand them out to my teachers! Mom, you’re the best—I love you!
