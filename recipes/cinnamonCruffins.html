Cinnamon Sugar Cruffins
Best Homemade Recipe

Cinnamon Sugar Cruffins:

  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp yeast
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter

Cinnamon Sugar Mixture:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup softned butter

Instructions

1. Mix 2 ¼ cup flour + ½ tsp salt
2. In a separate bowl, mix ⅓ cup of milk, ⅓ cup of water, 2 tbsp of sugar, and 2tsp yeast
3. Next, add the yeast mixture to the flour mixture until the dough is smooth
4. Add 1/4 cup of butter
5. Shape into a ball and rise for an hour or until doubled in size
6. Divide into 6 parts
7. Mix together the 1/2 cup of sugar and 1/2 tbsp of cinnamon to amke the cinnamon sugar mixture
8. Roll a dough ball out, spread butter and cinnamon mixture on top and roll it together
9. Cut the long rolled up dough in half and twist it into a rose like shape
10. Butter your muffin pan and place each dough into each muffin compartment, repeat for all 6 parts of dough, you should be able to make
11. cruffins since you cut each in half
12. Rise for 1 hour so the cruffins are doubled in size
13. Bake at 440F for 14 minutes. Enjoy!!
