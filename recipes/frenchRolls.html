Fluffy Buttery Petit French Rolls |
Beginner Friendly

Petit French Rolls:

  • 1 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • Pinch of salt

♡♡♡♡♡♡

  • 1/2 cup of water(110 F)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Extra butter for topping

Instructions

1. Mix 1/2 cup warm water, 1 tsp yeast, and 2 tsp sugar, let it rest for 15 minutes
2. In a separate bowl, mix together 1 1/4 cup flour and a pinch of salt
3. Add the yeast mixture with the dry ingredients and mix, then add the butter, and mix
4. When the dough is smooth, rest for 45 minutes, covered in plastic wrap or a towel
5. Divide the dough into 6 even pieces and roll each into desired shape
6. Put the balls on parchment paper, with space to rise, and rest for 35 minutes
7. Make a cut across the top and add butter in the cut
8. Bake at 440 F for 14 minutes and enjoy!
