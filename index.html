CHEF KATELYN

I'm Katelyn, a high school student with a strong passion for baking and cooking. In the summer of 2023, I started a YouTube channel, Chef Katelyn! My videos feature my favorite recipes along with covers of my favorite songs. In the fall of 2024, I launched my instagram account, @chef.katelyn. In the spring of 2025, I became obsessed with making custom royal icing sugar cookies. After gifting a Mother’s Day Cookie Box to my teacher, her referral led to my first business opportunity, launching my Chef Katelyn business with baby shower cookies as my initial venture. They bring me huge satisfaction when I see the happiness they spark in others. Custom cookies are very special because they connect people in a personal and meaningful way. I am truly happy that I can utilize my art and baking skills to bring sweetness and joy to others.

Lilo & Stitch Meringues
